Recipe #1 Beshparmak

  • Time: 1.5 hour
  • Serves: 8 portions
Ingredients Quantity
Dough 1-3kg
Meat 2-4kg
Onion 1-3
Carrot 1-3

Step 1: Place the meat, 1 peeled onion, and carrots in a saucepan. Cover with 2 liters of water.

Step 2: Bring to a boil and skim off any foam. Cover and simmer for 2-3 hours over low heat.

Step 3: Divide the dough into 3 parts.

Step 4: Cook the dough rectangles or diamonds in boiling broth until done, in 2-3 batches, for about 2 minutes each.
